What information does the Application collect and how is it used?
Information provided by the user

The app does not require user accounts, and users are not required to provide personal information.


Third-party data collection

  • Google AdMob: Collects data for ad delivery, performance, and personalized ads based on user preferences.
  • Expo: May collect technical information to monitor app performance.

User privacy preferences

  • Analytics tracking: Users can enable or disable analytics tracking in the app’s privacy settings. Analytics tracking is disabled by default, and users must explicitly opt-in for Firebase Analytics to collect data from their device. When tracking is disabled, Firebase Analytics will not collect any data from the device.
  • Personalized ads: Users can disable personalized ads in the app’s privacy settings. When disabled, users will still see ads, but they will not be tailored to their interests, and may be less relevant. The app will continue to function normally with non-personalized ads.

Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?

This application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.
