Privacy Policy - 4 Pictures 1 Word Solutions

This privacy policy applies to the mobile app "4 Pictures 1 Word Solutions" (referred to as "Application") developed by Sirish Shrestha (referred to as "Service Provider") as an ad-supported service. The service is provided "AS IS."

What Information Does the Application Collect and How Is It Used?

Information Provided by Users

The app does not require user accounts or the submission of personal information.

Data Collected by Third Parties

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized device data, including app usage and events, to understand user behavior and improve functionality.
  • Google AdMob: Collects data for serving ads, ad performance, and personalized ads based on user preferences.
  • Expo: May collect technical information to monitor app performance.

User Privacy Preferences

  • Analytics Tracking: Users can enable or disable analytics tracking in the app's privacy settings. When disabled, Firebase Analytics stops data collection for that device.
  • Personalized Ads: Users can disable personalized ads in the privacy settings. Non-personalized ads will still appear but may be less relevant.

Real-Time Location Information

The app does not collect precise real-time location information from your device.

GDPR Compliance

Rights as EU Citizens: Users in the EU have GDPR rights, including access, correction, deletion, and restriction of data processing. Since the app does not directly collect personal data, users can contact support for third-party-related requests.

Contact Support: To request data access or deletion, contact sirish@pictures-of-nepal.com with your Advertising ID or Instance ID for identification purposes.

Third-Party Links

Clicking on a word from the answer list may open a third-party dictionary website within the app. These websites are not controlled by the service provider. Users are encouraged to review the respective privacy policies.

Sharing Collected Information

Aggregated, anonymized data may be shared with third parties to improve the app. Information may be disclosed:

  1. When required by law.
  2. In good faith for user safety or fraud prevention.
  3. With trusted service providers adhering to this privacy policy.

Opt-Out Options

Users can stop all data collection by uninstalling the app using standard device procedures.

Data Retention and Management

The service provider retains data for the duration of app usage and for a reasonable period afterward. Automatically collected data is retained for up to 24 months. To delete data, contact sirish@pictures-of-nepal.com.

Children

The app is not intended for users under 13. No personal data is knowingly collected from children under 13. If discovered, such data will be deleted immediately. Parents or guardians can contact sirish@pictures-of-nepal.com to request deletion of such data.

Security

The service provider takes measures to protect your data, including limiting access to authorized personnel. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Effective Date

This policy is effective as of November 11, 2024.

Contact

For questions about data security or to request data removal, contact sirish@pictures-of-nepal.com.
