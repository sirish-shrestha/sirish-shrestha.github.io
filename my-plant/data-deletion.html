Data Deletion Instructions

MyPlant (iOS/Android) — Effective Date: [2026-02-02]

This page explains how to request deletion of your MyPlant account and personal data.

Summary

MyPlant currently supports data deletion requests via email. If you signed in using Google or Facebook, you can request deletion of your MyPlant account and associated data by following the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to: sirish_shr@yahoo.com
  2. Use the subject line: MyPlant Data Deletion Request
  3. In the email body, include:
    • Your MyPlant account email address (the one used for Google/Facebook login)
    • Your full name (optional but helpful)
    • A clear request to delete your account and data

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, we will delete (or anonymize where appropriate) data associated with your account, such as:

  • Account identifiers (e.g., name, email, provider user ID used for login)
  • Your plant list and reminder schedules (watering, pruning, fertilizing, repotting, etc.)
  • Your uploaded plant photos stored within MyPlant (if any)
  • Other content you created in the app (e.g., notes, if applicable)

What Data May Be Retained

In certain cases, we may retain limited information if required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes (for example, records needed to comply with law). If retained, we will keep it only as long as necessary and protect it.

Verification and Timeline

For your security, we may ask you to verify ownership of the account (for example, by emailing from the same address used to sign in). We aim to respond and complete deletion within a reasonable time after verification.

Removing MyPlant from Facebook (Optional)

If you used Facebook Login, you can also remove MyPlant’s access from your Facebook settings. This stops future data sharing from Facebook to MyPlant, but it does not automatically delete data already stored in MyPlant. To delete stored data, please follow the steps above.

Contact

If you have questions about deletion, contact: sirish_shr@yahoo.com
